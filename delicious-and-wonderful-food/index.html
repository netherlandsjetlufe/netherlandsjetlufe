The cooking surface of the cooker consists of several burners for installing dishes on them. In gas and electric plates, each burner is controlled by a separate handle. Usually, the burners have a different diameter and different maximum power. It is necessary to install dishes of different sizes on them, without fear of significant energy losses and damage to parts of the dishes that are not designed for high temperatures.

According to the execution, the cookers can be made in the form of a separate unit or built-in. Built-in plates usually have a separate cooking surface and an oven.
